About Andara Signal

Methodology · v1.5-NG

Andara Signal monitors the operational health of African carriers using independently verifiable operational proxies — schedule integrity, aircraft utilisation, fuel payment behaviour, ground handler billing, regulatory compliance, yield signals, infrastructure charge payments, and settlement behaviour. It produces a continuous directional verdict updated as new proxy data arrives, with a human committee gate required on any band-crossing change.

Confidence is computed, never analyst-tagged. Three inputs: coverage (live proxies as a share of applicable proxies, weighted 0.50), freshness (mean days since last live-proxy refresh, weighted 0.30), and sign agreement (share of live proxies whose sub-score matches the composite's direction, weighted 0.20). Thresholds: ≥ 0.80 → High, ≥ 0.55 → Medium, ≥ 0.30 → Low, < 0.30 or fewer than 2 live proxies → Insufficient. Hover any confidence pill to see the inputs that drove it.

Predictive accuracy on the downside signal: 70–80%. Carriers that move to Deteriorating subsequently show verifiable payment or operational stress within the indicated forward window. Signal tells you what is happening to a carrier's operational and financial health before the carrier tells you. AAV · Andara Analytical View

The 5-Score Methodology

Five weighted dimensions, five distinct questions. Weighted average produces a 0–100 composite. Verdicts are calculated outputs — never analyst opinions. The methodology is geography-agnostic and applies uniformly across all African regions.

SVS · 15%
Strategic Value
Weight 15 percent
Does this airline make African aviation stronger?
  • Network strategic importance
  • Route monopoly / connectivity
  • Employment footprint
  • Government / sovereign relationship
  • Brand equity and recognition
  • Years in continuous operation
  • Domestic market share
  • IATA, AFRAA membership
  • SAATM / ICAO contribution
PPS · 25%
Payment Priority
Primary creditworthiness
When times are hard, will they pay first?
  • Lessor payment history
  • MRO vendor payment
  • Fuel supplier payment
  • Regulator fee compliance
  • Tax / PAYE compliance
  • Bank facility servicing
  • Cash position / buffers
  • Revenue trend (3–5 yr)
APS · 20%
Airline Performance
Operating discipline
Are they running a tight operation?
  • IOSA registration
  • AOC currency
  • USOAP CMA proxy
  • Accident / incident record
  • Average fleet age
  • Fleet commonality
  • Enforcement record
  • SAFA / SACA findings
  • In-house ATO / AMO
RCS · 25%
Risk Containment
Structural protections
If things go wrong, how protected are we?
  • Cape Town Convention
  • IDERA practice
  • Repossession case law
  • FX repatriation risk
  • Ownership transparency
  • Audited financials
  • Insurance coverage
  • Country-level RCS modifier
RAS · 15%
Redeployment Suitability
Asset liquidity
If they default, how fast can we move the asset?
  • Aircraft type liquidity
  • Engine type liquidity
  • Configuration heaviness
  • AD / SB compliance
  • Technical records
  • C-check / D-check status
  • African operator demand
  • Re-export timeline

Verdict thresholds

Hard floors apply
≥75DEPLOY — Counterparty meets institutional standard. Standard covenant package suffices.
50–74WATCH — Conditional support. Structured protections, monitoring triggers, reduced tenor required.
<50DO NOT DEPLOY — Decline. Explanatory memo to Credit Committee mandatory.
Hard floorComposite cannot exceed 65 if PPS < 40 or RCS < 40, regardless of other scores. Exercised on 7 carriers across the combined universe.
